All About Lions

😀 Introduction 😀

Lions are the second biggest big cat to the tiger, and are famous for being a predator. They hunt at night and live in Africa, but a small population of them live in the Gir Forest in India. Lions have lived in Europe, Asia, and Africa but now they only live in Africa a a small part of India. Lions live in groups called Prides that can consist of up to 47 members but the average size is 15.

😀 Where they live 😀

Lions live in a variety of areas including open plains, thick brush areas, or dry forests. Though a small poulation of lions live in India's Gir Forest. The rest of lions live from the Southern Sahara to Northern South Africa. Though they don't live in the rainforests.

😀 What they Eat 😀

Lions usally eat zebra and wildbeast but if there is a shortage smaller animals and reptiles will do. Thye also might steal kills from other animals like hyenas and leopards. A lion might also eat livstock from local villages.

😀 Family Group 😀

Lions live in prides consisting of females that stay there for life, and a adult male/males. When male lions reach adulthood they are forced out and often form a new group with thier cousins and brothers. The amount of food avaliable determines how big a pride will be. If food is low there will be less lions in a pride. If food is really low sometimes females forced out to find a new pride.

😀 Conservation Status 😀

Lion population has declined in the past decade by 30 percent. It is estimated that 20,000 lions remain. Habitat loss, posioning, and hunting are some of the dangers lions face. As well as domestic dogs that live in the villages spread distemper which kills the lions. The lions that live in the Gir forest suffer from having a small habitat and frequenting coming in contact with people. They are threatened by growing infustructure, so they need more habitat to grow the poulation. You can help by while you are on vacation by makeing sure to volunteer and help the different organizations helping the lions. You can also help by not bying animal prducts becuase this encourages illegal wildlife trade.

😀 Fun Facts 😀

Baby lions start off with spots that disapper as they mature. Lions are very adaptable animals and can even get water from plants in reall dry areas. Lions sometimes like to hunt during storms becuase it is harder for their prey to hear them.
