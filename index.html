All About Lions

Introduction

Lions are the second biggest big cat to the tiger, and are famous for being a predator. THe hunt at night and live in Africa and a small population of them live in the Gir Forest in India. Lions have lived in Europe, Asia, and Africa but now they only live in Africa a a smalll part of India. Lions live in groups called Prides that can consist of up to 47 members but the average siz is 15.

Where they live

Lions live in a variety of areas like open plains, thick brush areas, or dry forests. Though a small poulation of lions live in India's Gir Forest. The rest of lions live from the Southern Sahara to Northern South Africa. Though they don't live in the rainforests.

What they Eat

Lions usally eat zebra and wildbeast but if there is a shortage smaller animals and reptiles will do. Thye also might steal kills from other animals like hyenas and leopards. A lion might also eat livstock from local villages.

Conservation Status

Lion population has declined in the past decade by 30 percent. It is estimated that 20,000 lions remain. Habitat loss, posioning, and hunting are some of the dangers lions face. As well as domestic dogs that live in the villages spread distemper which kills the lions. The lions that live in the Gir forest suffer from having a small habitat and frequenting coming in contact with people. They are threatened by growing infustructure, so they need more habitat to grow the poulation.
